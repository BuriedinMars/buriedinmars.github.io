##How to write a *Headling* 
use a '#' and there are 6 levels of headlings.
***Examples:***
  ###Title level 3
  ####Title level 4
  #Title level 1
  
##Word duractions
'**' for Bold 
  **This is bold.**
'*' for Italic
  *This is italic.*
'>' for quote

##List style
use '-' or numbers.
***Examples:***
-List element 1
-List element 2
-List element 3
